🔍 Project Overview

This freelance project for Buy3Get1 started as a simple site cleanup — but quickly evolved into a high-impact transformation. The business didn’t have an inventory page before I joined. I designed and launched one from scratch, organizing hundreds of products and making them accessible online for the first time. The client later told me that a customer actually came into the store and asked about a product they found on the new inventory page — direct proof that the work had an immediate impact.

I used AI tools like ChatGPT as a learning accelerator, teaching myself WordPress, Elementor, plugin structure, and SEO fundamentals in real-time — solving real problems under pressure and delivering fast results.

💼 Responsibilities

  • Inventory Import: Imported and organized hundreds of scraped Facebook Marketplace listings into the WordPress store — optimizing titles, categories, and layout for UX and SEO.
  • Web Scraping: Built custom scraping logic to pull product data, images, and titles for fast inventory management.
  • SEO Implementation: Installed and configured Yoast SEO, rewrote metadata and cleaned URLs to improve search indexing and visibility.
  • UI Improvements: Cleaned up layouts, optimized mobile views, adjusted fonts, added filters, and streamlined product listings.
  • WordPress Self-Learning: Rapidly learned the WordPress ecosystem, Elementor page builder, plugin workflows, and content structure.
  • AI-Accelerated Learning: Used ChatGPT to understand WordPress concepts, troubleshoot plugin issues, and speed up technical SEO and design work.
  • Inventory Management System: Created a reusable spreadsheet template for the client to import future products, update listings, and manage inventory with minimal effort.

🚀 Phase 2: The Redesign & Growth

Following the success of the initial inventory system, I was brought back for a complete site-wide redesign. The goal was to modernize the brand, improve user engagement, and optimize for long-term growth.

✨ New Features & Strategic Improvements

  • Elementor Integration: Migrated the site to Elementor to provide a highly customizable and maintainable foundation for the client.
  • Company Slider: Added a dynamic showcase of partner companies to build instant social proof.
  • Google Reviews Integration: Pulled reviews onto the site to boost trust and conversion rates.
  • FAQ Page: Researched and implemented a comprehensive FAQ section to improve SEO and reduce support inquiries.
